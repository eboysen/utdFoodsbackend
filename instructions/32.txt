Combine graham cracker and ¼ cup of melted butter. Press into a greased 8” pie pan to form crust
, Beat softened cream cheese until fluffy, Gradually blend in sugar, lemon juice, vanilla, and salt, Add eggs one at a time beating well, Pour filling into crust, Bake at 325o for 25-30 minutes or until set, For cream cheese topping combine sour cream, 2 Tbsp of sugar and ½ tsp vanilla. Alternatively 1 can of cherry pie filling. Spread on top of cake carefully, Bake 10 more minutes, Cool for several hours before serving
