Prepare diced onion, diced bell peppers, and browned ground beef, Open all canned goods, Cook the meat and onion at medium-high heat until meat is brown then add the bell peppers, When the bell peppers are soft enough to break with a spatula add the rest of the ingredients
, Bring the soup to a boil. Reduce heat to low and let simmer for 30 minutes, Serve with avocado cheese sour cream cilantro and tortilla chips
