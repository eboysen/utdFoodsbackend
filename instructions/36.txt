Cut butter and add it to a large mixing bowl with the flour and salt. Using either a pastry knife or a fork and knife break up the butter into smaller pieces and coat in flour until they are pea sized, Add cold water to the dry ingredients and mix thoroughly until a loose flaky dough is formed, Divide the dough into 3 balls and place in the fridge for at least 30 minutes, Preheat oven to 400 degrees, Roll out one dough ball until it is about 1/16" thick, Cut into six equal sections, For each section put about ⅙ tbsp of butter 1 ½ tbsp of sugar and a hefty dash of cinnamon (1-2 pounds on the bottom of the bottle)
, Fold each side of the dough over to seal. Place folded side down into a greased glass pie pan, Bake at 400 degrees for about 20 minutes or until golden brown and firm to the touch., Repeat for remaining balls of dough
